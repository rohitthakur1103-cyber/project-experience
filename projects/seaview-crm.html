Seaview Crabshack CRM System

A lightweight CRM and marketing operations layer built to unify customer data across fragmented systems and make weekly outreach more systematic.

  • Python
  • SQLite
  • CRM workflow
  • Marketing operations
Open live app

Core problem

Customer records, touchpoints, and outreach data were fragmented across exports, spreadsheets, and separate systems. The CRM is meant to sit on top of those systems and create one usable operating layer.

Current prototype capabilities

  • Centralizes customer profiles and purchase history in SQLite.
  • Imports CSV exports from legacy and fragmented systems.
  • Tracks touchpoints from website and in-person capture.
  • Supports audience segmentation and campaign planning.
  • Exports audience lists for current email or SMS tools.

Dashboard

Overview of CRM and marketing status across customers, segments, and recent activity.

Customers

Unified customer records with source, spend, tags, notes, and interaction history.

Marketing hub

Campaign planning, segment review, and export workflows for repeatable weekly outreach.

Imports and capture

Upload and lead-capture flows that keep the system current without replacing existing tools.

Demo access

The app is framed as staff-only software, but the current demo build uses a presentation gate. Any username and password will enter the app in demo mode.

What this is not

This is not a POS replacement. It is a CRM plus marketing operations layer designed to sit above the current systems and make customer follow-through easier.

What this shows: product thinking around real operating constraints, internal tooling design, and an ability to build software around messy business workflows.
